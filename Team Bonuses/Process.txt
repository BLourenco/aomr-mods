How to convert a Major God bonus into a Team Bonus.
----------------------------------------------------
1. Create a new Team Bonus Tech, flagged as a TeamTech and as Volatile
2. Add prerequisites of being the correct Major God and (as a workaround) having at least 1 villager alive so that the tech activates once the starting units are spawned.
3. Add the effects and message to the tech.
4. Remove the effect from the ArchaicAge tech for that specific God using mergeMode="remove".
